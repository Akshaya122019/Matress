Soft & Supportive Recron Pillow Collection

Kumar Industries offers premium Recron pillows designed for long-lasting comfort and support. Filled with high-quality Recron fiber, these pillows retain their shape, providing excellent neck and spine alignment for a restful sleep.

Manufactured in our in-house facility in Nagercoil, our Recron pillows are lightweight, breathable, and resistant to sagging, ensuring durability and plush comfort. Available in various sizes and firmness levels, they cater to different sleep preferences.

Upgrade your sleep experience with our ultra-soft and supportive Recron pillows—where comfort meets durability!
