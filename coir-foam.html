Natural Comfort Meets Modern Support: Coir with Foam Mattresses by Kumar Industries

Kumar Industries brings you the perfect fusion of nature and innovation with its Coir with Foam Mattresses, designed to provide firm support and unmatched comfort. The natural coir layer ensures optimal air circulation, keeping the mattress cool and fresh even during warm nights. Combined with high-density foam, it delivers excellent cushioning and pressure relief, supporting your spine and reducing discomfort for a healthier sleeping posture.

Crafted with eco-friendly and durable materials, our Coir with Foam Mattresses are naturally resistant to dust mites, bacteria, and allergens, making them a safe choice for your family. The robust design ensures long-lasting performance, while the breathable fabric cover adds an extra layer of comfort and hygiene. Ideal for those who value natural elements in their sleep experience, these mattresses offer a balanced blend of firm support and plush relaxation for a restful night's sleep.
